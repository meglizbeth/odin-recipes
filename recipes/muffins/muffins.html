Muffins

Description

This is a deliciously simple recipe for muffins that is egg free and low nickel. Enjoy a yummy and gentle start to your morning!

Ingredients

  • flour
  • sugar
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Line mini muffin pan with muffin liners.
  2. Mix dry ingredients together in bowl.
  3. Add melted butter and water. Stir thoroughly.
  4. Scoop muffin batter into muffin cups.
  5. Bake for 14 minutes.
  6. Let cool and enjoy.
