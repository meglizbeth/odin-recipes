Banana Bread

Description

This simple recipe for banana bread is big on taste! I have tinkered the recipe and bake time so that it comes out perfectly done and not burnt every time.

Ingredients

  • ripe bananas
  • flour
  • sugar
  • egg
  • butter
  • baking soda
  • salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees Fahrenheit. Line a 9 inch loaf pan with parchment paper.
  2. Using a stand mixer, mix bananas until they are mashed.
  3. Add sugar and egg.
  4. Add melted butter.
  5. Add dry ingredients and mix thoroughly.
  6. Pour into loaf pan.
  7. Bake for 65 minutes.
  8. Let cool and enjoy!
